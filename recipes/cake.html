Gluten Free Chocolate Cake

Description

This black bean chocolate cake is fudy, decadenbt, and completely gluten-free!

Ingredients

  • Black Beans
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cocoa
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Grease and line 2 6-inch round cake pans with parchment paper.
  2. In a high speed blender or food processor, puree the beans, sugar, oil, eggs and vanilla until completely smooth
  3. Add the cocoa powder, baking powder, and baking soda
  4. Divide batter equally into prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes
