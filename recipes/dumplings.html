Asian Dumpling Recipe

Description

The only dumpling recipe you'll ever need!

Ingredients

  • chives
  • ground pork
  • shaoxing wine
  • sesame oil
  • salt
  • soy sauce
  • white pepper
  • dumpling wrappers

Steps

  1. Chop chives finely.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the vegetables, wine, meat, sesame oil, salt, soy sauce, white pepper, and water.
  3. Wrap dumplings.
