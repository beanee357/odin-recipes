Korean Kimchi Pancakes

Description

Crispy, savory kimchi pancakes with a sweet and tangy soy dipping sauce - perfectly balanced, packed with flavor, and irresistibly satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Korean Pancake Mix
  • Icy Cold Water
  • Fermented Kimchi
  • Kimchi Liquid
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Combine mix and water
  2. Heat pan and add oil
  3. Scoop batter and pour into pan. Spread thin.
  4. Let pancake cook over medium heat for 10 to 20 seconds.
